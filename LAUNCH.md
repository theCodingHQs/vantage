## 2. Product Description (Conversion-Optimized)

Are you tired of switching between Notion pages, Google Sheets, Trello boards, FreshBooks, and Toggl timers? You are losing billable hours, forgetting proposal follow-ups, and presenting a disjointed experience to your high-paying clients.

**Vantage** gives you the edge. It is a premium, full-stack Freelancer Operating System designed to centralize your entire business operations. Vantage replaces 6-8 separate subscriptions with a single, lightning-fast workspace.

From the moment you register, the **onboarding wizard** initializes your company branding, currency, and defaults. The **CRM pipeline** tracks leads from proposal to checkout. Start project contracts, create task checklists, and trigger the **Zustand persistent live-timer** (which continues running even if you refresh your browser!).

When the job is complete, auto-generate branded, tax-ready invoices from your logged time entries, and copy secure **Client Portal** links where clients can review deliverables, view invoice history, and chat with you directly. 

No monthly enterprise fees. Own your data. Export your entire database as a JSON file anytime in one click. Deploy to Vercel, Railway, or VPS in under 10 minutes.

## 5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

- **Q: What is the stack?**  
  A: TanStack Start (React 19, isomorphic server functions, Vite), Drizzle ORM (PostgreSQL), and Redis (caching).
- **Q: Can I self-host this?**  
  A: Yes! Vantage is fully open-source and easy to deploy on Railway, Vercel, or any Node.js VPS.
- **Q: Is there any database lock-in?**  
  A: None. Your data is 100% yours. You can download your entire workspace backup as a JSON file in one click in settings.
- **Q: Do I need a paid Redis account?**  
  A: No. A free Upstash Redis instance (included in setup env) handles all caches and session stores.
- **Q: Does it support multiple currencies?**  
  A: Yes, you can specify USD, EUR, GBP, AUD, or CAD defaults during onboarding.
