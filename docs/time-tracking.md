# Time Tracking & Timesheets

Track billable hours with the persistent live timer or log timesheets manually.

---

## 1. Persistent Live Timer

- The live timer is positioned in the sidebar and **runs persistently across routes**.
- If you reload the browser, Vantage calculates the difference since the start timestamp and continues ticking accurately.
- Click **Stop Timer** to save the entry. The duration is rounded to the nearest minute.

---

## 2. Manual Timesheet Logging

- Click **Log Hours Manually** in the Time Tracker view.
- Select a project, task, date, and input hours and minutes.

---

## 3. CSV Data Exporter

- Click **Export CSV** in the Time Tracker view.
- Vantage generates a formatted CSV spreadsheet containing all descriptions, projects, dates, hours, and billable values, and triggers a download immediately.
