# Invoices & Billing Statements

Generate premium, branded PDF receipts and track outstanding income inside Vantage.

---

## 1. Invoice Builder

- Click **+ New Invoice** to open the builder.
- Select a client from the dropdown.
- Add line items (service, time, expense, product) with quantity and unit price. The subtotal and total calculate live.
- Input custom tax rates (%) or discount amounts.
- Save as draft.

---

## 2. Invoices List & Details

- Filter invoices by status: `Draft`, `Sent`, `Paid`, or `Overdue`.
- Click on an invoice to review its layout.
- Click the **Print Icon** to trigger browser print-to-PDF formatting (styled for clean letter printing).
- Click **Send Invoice** to simulate email delivery to the client.
- Click **Mark Paid** to record manual payment methods (Bank Wire, Cash, Credit Card) and transaction notes. This updates the client's total revenue instantly.
