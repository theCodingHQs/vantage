# Content Calendar & Self-Marketing

Use the Content Calendar to plan self-marketing posts across social channels.

---

## 1. Month-Grid Calendar

- Color-coded platform badges represent social platforms: Twitter/X (blue), LinkedIn (deep blue), Instagram (pink), YouTube (red), Blog (green), other (slate).
- Displays post title snippets on the scheduled calendar day.

---

## 2. Managing Posts

- Click on any day cell to open the post builder pre-populated with that date.
- Choose a platform, input copy text, internal distribution tags, and track progress via workflow status stages:
  - `Idea` (Backlog)
  - `Drafting`
  - `Scheduled`
  - `Published`
- Click on any scheduled post to edit details or delete the record.
