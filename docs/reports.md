# Reports & Analytics Dashboard

Analyze your freelance business performance, profit margins, and cash outlays inside Vantage.

---

## 1. Expense Allocation Chart

- A visual donut chart categories breakdown of all logged business outlays (Software, Travel, Equipment, Contractors, Marketing, Other).
- Tracks total cash outlays and billable outlays separately.

---

## 2. Project Profitability Ledger

- A summary table displaying:
  - Total project revenue (paid invoices).
  - Internal contractor/outlay costs.
  - Logged hours.
  - Net profits.
  - Profit margins (%).

---

## 3. Invoice Aging Report

- Aggregates outstanding invoices into standard aging buckets: Under 30 days, 30-60 days, 60-90 days, and Over 90 days.
- Helps identify delinquent client accounts to send manual payment reminders.
